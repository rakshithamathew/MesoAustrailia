Mesothelioma
Resources & Support:
The facts you need

  • Mesothelioma is a rare and aggressive cancer primarily caused by asbestos exposure, affecting the lungs, abdomen, or heart.
  • Asbestos, a naturally occurring mineral with heat-resistant properties, was widely used in construction, shipbuilding, and manufacturing.
  • Inhalation or ingestion of microscopic asbestos fibers causes them to lodge in the mesothelium, leading to inflammation, scarring, and genetic mutations.

Silent but deadly

Each year, more than 700 mesothelioma related deaths, with a mortality rate of 2.1 per 100,000 people is reported.

The cause is clear

Caused by asbestos exposure, often from workplace settings, home renovations, or environmental contact.

Symptoms take time

Persistent coughing, chest pain, and breathing difficulties can develop years after asbestos exposure. Early diagnosis is key to better treatment.

Legal rights matter

Victims may be entitled to compensation for medical expenses, lost income, and suffering. Seeking legal advice can make a real difference.
