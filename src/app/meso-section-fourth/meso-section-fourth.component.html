Symptoms

Caused by asbestos exposure, often from workplace settings, home renovations, or environmental contact.

Risk factors

Prolonged asbestos exposure, living near asbestos sites, and secondhand exposure from workers are primary risks, with rare genetic factors or radiation exposure also potentially linked.

Causes

Asbestos exposure can occur through environmental contact with air or soil, contaminated clothing from workers, or contaminated talc products, increasing the risk of related diseases.
