Pleural Mesothelioma

It is the most common type of mesothelioma, accounting for 75% of all cases. This aggressive cancer develops in the pleura, the thin membrane surrounding the lungs.

Peritoneal Mesothelioma

It is a rare but serious cancer that affects the peritoneum, the protective lining of the abdomen. This form of mesothelioma accounts for 15 20% of all cases and is caused by asbestos exposure

Pericardial Mesothelioma

Is an extremely rare form of mesothelioma, accounting for less than 1% of all cases. This aggressive cancer develops in the pericardium, the thin membrane surrounding the heart.
