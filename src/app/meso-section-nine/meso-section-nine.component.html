How do I know if I qualify for a mesothelioma lawsuit?

If you or a loved one has mesothelioma from asbestos exposure, you may be eligible. Key factors include your work history, exposure sources, and medical records.

What compensation can I receive for a mesothelioma case?

You may receive compensation for medical bills, lost wages, pain, suffering, and wrongful death claims.

How long do I have to file a mesothelioma claim?

The time limit to file a lawsuit depends on your state’s statute of limitations. Typically, it ranges from 1 to 5 years after diagnosis. It’s best to act fast and consult a legal expert. 

What happens after I submit my case for review?

Our legal team will assess your eligibility and connect you with a mesothelioma attorney then guide you through the process with no upfront cost.

What if the asbestos company is no longer in business?

Even if the responsible company has shut down, asbestos trust funds worth over $30 billion have been set up to compensate victims.

Will I have to go to court?

A skilled mesothelioma lawyer will handle the legal process, negotiating on your behalf and guiding you if a trial becomes necessary.
