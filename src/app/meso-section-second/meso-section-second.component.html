Who is at risk?

Each year, more than 800 people in Australia are diagnosed with mesothelioma. The median age at diagnosis is 77 years, with approximately two new cases identified daily.

Construction workers – Exposure in
insulation, drywall, roofing.
Shipyard workers – Asbestos used in ship
insulation.
Factory workers – Industrial machinery
contained asbestos.
Firefighters – Asbestos released from
burning buildings.
Military veterans (Navy, Army, Air Force) – Asbestos used in ships, barracks, and vehicles.
